By Ann Buonocore - August 10, 2014

Fascinating guests this week

The creator of the original "McDonald's Hands-on Business Training Program"

Joy E. Karp tells her first hand story of how her successful training manual was used to train employees world wide. Her program significantly reduced staff turnover and ultimately changed the way McDonald's trained and promoted crewmembers worldwide. AMAZING is the definition of this strong woman who opens up about her personal life including a serious car accident, two heart attacks, a quadruple heart by-pass operation, the trauma of a kidnapping and being buried alive for 16 hours, PTSD and cancer yet all the while staying focused on her true passion: authentic service.
